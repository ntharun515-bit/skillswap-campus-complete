How SkillSwap Works

From project posting to payment release — a seamless four-step lifecycle designed for zero friction.

1

Post a Project

Clients describe their needs, set a budget in campus credits, and define the required skills and deadline.

2

Hire Verified Talent

Review proposals from skill-verified students, compare bids, and hire with a single click. Funds are locked in escrow instantly.

3

Collaborate in Workspace

Manage tasks on a Kanban board, share deliverables, chat in real-time, and track progress through the project timeline.

4

Review & Release Payment

Once work is submitted, review deliverables and approve. Credits are released from escrow to the freelancer instantly.
