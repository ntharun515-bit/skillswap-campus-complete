🎓 Our Story

Empowering Students to Earn,
Learn & Build — Together

SkillSwap was born from a simple idea: every university campus is full of untapped talent — coders, designers, writers, tutors — who can help each other grow while gaining real professional experience.

🎯

Our Mission

To create a secure, closed-campus marketplace where students can monetize their academic skills, build professional portfolios, and gain real-world freelance experience — all before graduating.

🔭

Our Vision

A future where every university student enters the workforce with a verified track record of completed projects, client reviews, and earned credentials — bridging the gap between academia and industry.

💡

The Problem We Solve

Traditional freelancing platforms aren't built for students. They lack trust verification, campus context, and academic-friendly workflows. SkillSwap fills that gap with peer-authenticated, escrow-protected collaboration.

Our Core Values

🤝

Trust First

Every transaction is protected by smart escrow. Every freelancer can be skill-verified. Every interaction is logged. Trust isn't assumed — it's engineered.

🎓

Student-Centric Design

Built for academic schedules, campus budgets, and student skill levels. No hidden fees, no corporate gatekeeping — just peer-to-peer collaboration.

🏆

Meritocratic Growth

Your reputation is earned through completed projects, client reviews, XP milestones, and skill verifications — not bought or gamed.

🔓

Radical Transparency

Open project budgets, public reviews, visible escrow status, and full activity audit logs. Every stakeholder sees the same truth.
